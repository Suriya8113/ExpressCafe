About Us

Welcome to Express Cafe where every cup tells a story. We're passionate about serving the finest coffee, crafted from carefully selected beans and brewed to perfection. Frequently Asked Questions

What makes Express Café unique? +
Express Café stands out with its cozy ambiance, premium coffee sourced from local roasters, and a menu featuring freshly baked pastries and light bites, perfect for a quick stop or a relaxed meetup.
What are the operating hours of Express Café? +
Express Café is open Monday through Friday from 7:00 AM to 8:00 PM, and Saturday through Sunday from 8:00 AM to 6:00 PM.
Does Express Café offer vegan or gluten-free options? +
Yes, we offer a variety of vegan and gluten-free pastries, sandwiches, and beverages. Check our menu board or ask our staff for daily specials!
Can I reserve a table at Express Café? +
We operate on a first-come, first-served basis, but you can call ahead to check availability for small groups during off-peak hours.
Does Express Café offer free Wi-Fi? +
Yes, Express Café provides free high-speed Wi-Fi for all customers, perfect for working, studying, or browsing while enjoying your coffee.
